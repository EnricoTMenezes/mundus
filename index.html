About Mundus

Mundus publishes books, ebooks, and audiobooks, leveraging human-AI cooperation to bring literature to readers worldwide.

We focus on translations to 10+ languages and have published over 1,000 public domain books, making classic literature accessible to everyone.

Our mission is to break down language barriers and preserve literary heritage through innovative publishing methods.
